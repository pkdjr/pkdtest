About Us

Our esteemed company traces its origins back to the 1950s when it was established as The Western India Fibre Company under the visionary leadership of the late Mr. P.V. Kesavan, an ardent industrialist and pioneer in the coir industry. Today, under the stewardship of his younger son, Mr. P.K. Dinesh, this company stands as a tribute to Mr. Kesavan’s enduring legacy.


With deep roots in the rich tradition of the coir industry, we are proud custodians of a heritage that spans over seven decades. Our unwavering dedication to excellence is reflected in every product we craft, each embodying the perfect synergy of time-honored craftsmanship and modern innovation. We have set the gold standard in quality, consistently pushing the boundaries of innovation while upholding the highest principles of sustainability and integrity.

Crafting Heritage and Innovation in every Coir Product

Finely Handcrafted

Each product is made from handpicked coir fibers, carefully crafted and inspected at every stage to ensure exceptional quality and durability. With a focus on detail and precision, we deliver timeless, sustainable products that meet the highest standards.

Versatile Collections

We offer a wide range of products, including Doormats, Rugs, Broadloom, Green Solutions, and more, crafted to meet customer needs with exceptional quality. Crafted from high-quality materials, each product ensures durability, style and satisfaction.

Legacy of Excellence

With decades of expertise, we combine craftsmanship and innovation to deliver durable, eco-friendly coir products. Our commitment to sustainability and customer satisfaction sets industry standards, ensuring every product reflects our legacy of excellence and quality.

Sustainable Sensibility

Our products are crafted with a commitment to eco-friendly practices and sustainable sourcing. Made from renewable and biodegradable coir fibers and peat, they embody harmony with nature while ensuring minimal environmental impact.
